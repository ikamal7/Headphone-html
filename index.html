Why are wireless headphones so popular?

Wireless headphones have finally become a viable option. In previous years, the sound quality was much worse with bluetooth headphones than ones with cords. They would drain music player batteries and the sound wasn't even that great Nowadays, both of those problems are solved. Recent advances in Bluetooth technology have improved sound quality so much that the cord is no longer necessary for crisp and clear audio. Bluetooth still requires a deal of battery power, but longer battery lives of devices and the prevalence of portable chargers leave that concern in the past, too.
